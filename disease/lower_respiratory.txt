<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Lower Respiratory Tract Infections (LRTI) are infections that affect the airways (below the level of the larynx), including the trachea and the alveolar sacs.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>Communicable disease</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<p>They are carried in tiny droplets and passed between people by coughing, sneezing, and at times by indirect contact with surfaces.</p>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Fever</li>
  <li>Severe cough</li>
  <li>Chest pain or tightness</li>
  <li>Rapid breathing or difficulty breathing</li>
</ul>
