<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Meningitis is an infection of the meninges, which are the membranes surrounding the brain and spinal cord.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>Communicable disease</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Commonly affects children</li>
  <li>Cased by enteroviruses (common stomach viruses)</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Fever</li>
  <li>Purple-red skin rash or bruising</li>
  <li>Tiredness and drowsiness</li>
  <li>Irritability</li>
</ul>
