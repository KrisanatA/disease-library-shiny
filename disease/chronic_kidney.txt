<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Chronic kidney disease is also known as renal disease. It occurs when the kidneys are damaged and do not function as they should for a period longer than three months.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Diabetes</li>
  <li>People with kidney disease in the family</li>
  <li>People in the older age group</li>
  <li>People with high blood pressure</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>High blood pressure</li>
  <li>Nausea and vomiting</li>
  <li>Changes in the number of times you pass urine and how the urine looks</li>
</ul>
