<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Malaria is a disease that resulted from the bites of infected female Anopheles mosquitoes causing parasites to be transmitted to people.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<p>People with low immunity</p>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Fever</li>
  <li>Headache and chills</li>
  <li>Severe illness</li>
</ul>
