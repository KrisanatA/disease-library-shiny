<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Chronic respiratory diseases are diseases that affect the airways and other structures of the lungs. The most common disease are chronic obstructive pulmonary disease, asthma, and occupational lung diseases.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Tobacco smoke</li>
  <li>Occupational chemicals</li>
  <li>Dust</li>
  <li>Air pollution</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Breathlessness</li>
  <li>Wheezing (Asthma)</li>
</ul>
