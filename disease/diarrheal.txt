<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Diarrheal diseases in infections caused by a host of bacterial, viral, and parasitic organisms, most of which are spread by faces - contaminated water.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>Communicable disease</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Contaminated consuming water</li>
  <li>Poor sanitation environment</li>
  <li>Malnutrition</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Dehydration (loose or watery stool)</li>
  <li>Restlessness, irritability</li>
  <li>Sunken eyes</li>
</ul>
