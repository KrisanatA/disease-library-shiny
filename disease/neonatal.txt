<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Neonatal disorders are severe neurological problems that occur in babies. It ranges from conditions that affect the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, and muscles.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>Communicable disease</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<p>It can happen in the baby at birth or shortly after</p>

<h4><b>Impacts:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>The inflection in the mother's genital tract</li>
  <li>Lack of oxygen</li>
  <li>Physical injury to the head that causes bleeding inside the brain</li>
</ul>
