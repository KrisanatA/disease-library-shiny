<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Nutritional deficiencies are known as malnutrition, the result when someone has insufficient nutrients such as vitamins and minerals for their needs.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Lack of micronutrients such as iron and vitamin C consumption</li>
  <li>People with condition that prevents them from absorbing nutrients such as the elderly with dementia or dental problems</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Lose weight and muscle</li>
  <li>Pales skin</li>
  <li>Hairloss</li>
</ul>
