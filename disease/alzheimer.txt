<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Alzheimer's disease is a common form of dementia. It is a physical brain condition that disrupts the brain's neurons impacting how they work and communicate with each other.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

More common in older age groups such as those above the 50s

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Persistent and frequent short-term memory loss</li>
  <li>Language and comprehension difficulties</li>
  <li>Change in behaviour, personality, and mood</li>
  <li>Increasing disorientation in time, place, and person</li>
</ul>
