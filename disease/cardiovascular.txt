<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Cardiovascular diseases are a group of diseases that affect the heart and blood vessels, for example, high blood pressure; heart attack; and stroke.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Unhealthy diet</li>
  <li>Physical inactivity</li>
  <li>Tobacco use</li>
  <li>Harmful use of alcohol</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Pain or discomfort in the center of the chest</li>
  <li>Pain or discomfort in the arms, the left shoulder, elbows, jaw, or back</li>
  <li>Difficulty in breathing</li>
  <li>Sudden weakness of the face, arm, or leg, most often on one side of the body</li>
</ul>
