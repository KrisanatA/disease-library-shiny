<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Protein-Energy malnutrition is a deficiency of all macronutrients leading to sudden or gradual energy deficit.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Inadequate nutrient intake</li>
  <li>Impacts from disorders or drugs that interfere with nutrient use</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Weight loss</li>
  <li>Growth retardation</li>
  <li>Dry, thin, and wrinkled skin</li>
</ul>
