<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Maternal disorders are the group of pregnancy, childbirth, and puerperium complications. An example of maternal diseases includes diabetes, anemia, and asthma.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Age</li>
  <li>Pre-existing health issues such as diabetes, high blood pressure</li>
</ul>

<h4><b>Causes:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Severe bleeding (bleeding after childbirth)</li>
  <li>High blood pressure (pre-eclampsia and eclampsia)</li>
  <li>Infections (usually after childbirth)</li>
  <li>Complications from delivery</li>
  <li>Unsafe abortion</li>
</ul>
