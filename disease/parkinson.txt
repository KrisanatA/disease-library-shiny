<h4><b>Definition:</b></h4>

<p>Parkinson's disease is a disorder due to the damage to the nerve cells that produce dopamine, a chemical that is vital for the smooth control of muscles and movements.</p>

<h4><b>Disease category:</b></h4>

<p>NCD (Non-Communicable Disease)</p>

<h4><b>Risk factors:</b></h4>

<p>Mainly impacts people aged over 56.</p>

<h4><b>Symptoms:</b></h4>

<ul>
  <li>Tremor or shaking, often when resting or tired</li>
  <li>Limit movement from muscle rigidity or stiffness</li>
  <li>Stooped posture and balance problems</li>
</ul>
