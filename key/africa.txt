<h4><b>Afica:</b></h4>
<ul>
  <li>In the African region <em>HIV/AIDS, Neonatal disorders, Diarrheal diseases, Lower respiratory infections, Enteric infections, and Malaria</em>, these diseases have high mortality rates such that these need to be implemented:
</li>
    <ul>
      <li>Better access to a necessity, e.g. clean water, food</li>
      <li>Information on the disease should be more accessible, e.g. how to prevent getting the diseases</li>
      <li>Better accessibility to the medicines and treatment</li>
    </ul>
</ul>
